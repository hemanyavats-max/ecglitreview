01 Research Problem

The Gap Between
Precision and
Portability

Cardiovascular diseases remain the leading cause of death globally yet ECG machines stay locked inside hospitals. If you're at home, at work, or in a village with no hospital nearby, nobody is watching your heart.

High-accuracy deep learning models require GPU-level hardware, while traditional algorithms on microcontrollers sacrifice classification breadth. The need of the hour is a system advanced enough to identify specific arrhythmias AND small enough to run on a cheap chip in real time.
